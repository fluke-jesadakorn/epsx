# EPSX Business Requirements

## 1. Project Purpose & Goals

### Main Business Objective
Enable users to buy and sell stocks effortlessly using our team's strategic guidance without requiring investment knowledge.

**Primary Focus**: EPS growth-based trading strategies with expansion to all asset classes and business opportunities in the future.

### Target Users
- **Primary**: Investors seeking strategic guidance
- **Secondary**: Users interested in investment/financial sectors
- **Goal**: Democratize investment access through data-driven strategies

### Problem Statement
Current investment platforms require extensive knowledge. EPSX solves this by providing:
- Strategic guidance without knowledge requirements
- EPS growth-focused recommendations
- Simplified buy/sell experience through subscriptions

## 2. Scope & Core Features

### Core Features (MVP)
- **Subscription-based stock recommendations**
  - Easy stock visibility from subscription feed
  - One-click buy/sell based on our strategies
  - No investment knowledge required
- **Strategic guidance system**
  - EPS growth analysis and recommendations
  - Data-driven decision support
  - Automated strategic insights

### Future Features (Roadmap)
- **Platform expansion**
  - All asset classes (beyond stocks)
  - Business opportunity matching
- **Community features**
  - Fund manager and investor matching platform
  - Collaborative investment strategies

### Compliance Strategy
- **Legal positioning**: Avoid financial terminology
- **Technical framing**: Position as tech and data analysis platform
- **Risk mitigation**: Use data analysis wording instead of financial advice

## 3. Success Metrics

### Growth Targets
- **User base**: 20,000 users
- **Scalability**: Plugin integration with other platforms
- **Market expansion**: Multi-asset class support

### Performance Requirements
- **Reliability**: High uptime and data accuracy
- **Speed**: Not real-time but consistently reliable
- **User experience**: Simplified, knowledge-free interface

### Business KPIs
- User adoption rate
- Subscription conversion
- Platform integration success
- Strategy performance accuracy

## 4. Market Position

### Competitive Advantage
- Strategy-first approach vs. tool-first
- Knowledge-barrier elimination
- EPS growth specialization
- Future-ready platform architecture

### Market Strategy
- Start with stock market focus
- Expand to comprehensive investment platform
- Build ecosystem for investment community

## 5. Regulatory Considerations

### Legal Framework
- Position as technology/data analysis platform
- Avoid financial advisory terminology
- Ensure compliance through technical language
- Plan for regulatory scalability

### Risk Management
- Clear user disclaimers
- Technology-focused positioning
- Data-driven approach emphasis
- Educational content focus

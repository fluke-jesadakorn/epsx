# Welcome to EPSX

We're excited to launch **EPSX**, a new platform that brings together web3 analytics and stablecoin payments on Binance Smart Chain.

## What is EPSX?

EPSX is a microservices platform built from the ground up in Rust, with:

- **On-chain subscriptions** — Per-merchant vaults for predictable stablecoin revenue
- **Visual page builder** — Drag-and-drop editor with web3-native authentication
- **Paymaster gas sponsorship** — Users pay with USDC, never need BNB for gas
- **Multi-chain ready** — BSC today, Ethereum/Arbitrum/Base/Polygon tomorrow

## Why BSC?

BSC gives us:
- **Low gas** (~$0.05 per transaction)
- **Fast blocks** (3s)
- **Mature stablecoin ecosystem** (USDT, USDC, BUSD)
- **Large user base**

Sign in with your wallet to explore the platform. No email required, no KYC for basic features.
